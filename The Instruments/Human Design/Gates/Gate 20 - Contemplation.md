# Gate 20: Contemplation (The Now)

## Ra's Definition
According to Ra Uru Hu, Gate 20 is located in the Throat Center and represents **awareness in the now, expressed through action**—the capacity to be fully present in the moment and to manifest that presence through voice and behavior. This is not "contemplation" in the sense of passive meditation, but rather **active recognition of what is happening right now**, articulated and embodied.

Ra taught that Gate 20 operates within the **Integration Circuit**, which means it carries **individual empowerment expressed through present-moment awareness**. This gate enables the individual to recognize the now and to *speak* or *act* from that recognition—but this awareness is existential, rooted in the lived experience of being here, in this body, in this moment.

**"Gate 20 is the voice of the now. It's not about thinking about the past or planning the future—it's about recognizing what's happening right now and expressing it. This is the gate of presence made manifest. You see what is, and you say it, you embody it. But it's Integration circuit, so it's about your presence empowering you, not leading others."** — Ra Uru Hu[^1]

The keynote here is **existential awareness in the now**. This is the Throat Center's capacity to express **self-assurance** born from being fully present, from recognizing reality as it is and manifesting that recognition through voice and action.

## I-Ching Hexagram Analysis

Hexagram 20, **觀 (Guān) - Contemplation / View**, is composed of Wind (trigram Xun) above Earth (trigram Kun). This creates the image of **wind moving over the earth**—gentle, penetrating observation that sees all without forcing, that witnesses reality as it unfolds.

**Judgment (Wilhelm/Baynes):**
> "Contemplation. The ablution has been made, but not yet the offering. Full of trust they look up to him."

The I-Ching reveals that **true contemplation is preparation for sacred action**—not passive observation but *witnessing* that prepares one to act from truth. When Wind (penetration, gentle persistence) moves above Earth (receptivity, manifestation), the result is **deep seeing** that honors what is and prepares to respond.

**Image:**
> "The wind blows over the earth: the image of Contemplation. Thus the kings of old visited the regions of the world, contemplated the people, and gave them instruction."

The trigram structure (Wind above Earth) suggests that **contemplation serves manifestation**—the capacity to *observe* deeply in order to *act* wisely. This is not detachment but engagement, not withdrawal but presence that leads to instruction and expression.

**Nuclear Hexagram:** Hexagram 23 (Splitting Apart)—revealing that beneath contemplation lies **the necessity of breaking down false structures**, the recognition that true presence requires stripping away what obscures the now.

In Human Design terms, this hexagram's placement in the **Throat Center** transforms the I-Ching's cosmic observation into **expressed presence**. The question becomes: "Can I recognize what is happening right now and express that awareness through voice and action?"

## Line-by-Line Breakdown

### Line 1: Superficiality
**Exalted (Jupiter):** The capacity to recognize when awareness is superficial, when one is skimming the surface of the now rather than dwelling in it. Self-correcting presence.
**Detriment (Mars):** Remaining in superficial awareness, speaking and acting from surface impressions rather than deep recognition of the moment.

This line reveals that **presence begins with recognizing superficiality**—the awareness of when one is not truly in the now, when expression is mechanical rather than embodied. The individual with this line learns to distinguish between shallow observation and deep contemplation.

### Line 2: The Dogmatist
**Exalted (Saturn):** The wisdom to hold convictions about the now without rigidity, to recognize patterns while remaining open to each moment's uniqueness.
**Detriment (Neptune):** Dogmatic assertion that "this is how it is," imposing past frameworks on present reality, refusing to see what is actually happening now.

The second line bridges awareness (line 1) with expression. When the individual expresses the now without imposing dogma, their voice carries truth. When detriment, they speak from fixed ideas rather than present recognition.

### Line 3: Self-Awareness
**Exalted (Earth):** Deep recognition of self in the now—awareness of one's own presence, behavior, and impact in the moment. Grounded self-knowledge.
**Detriment (Uranus):** Self-awareness that becomes self-consciousness, observing oneself so intensely that spontaneous presence becomes impossible.

Line 3 is the trial-and-error foundation. This individual *experiments with presence* to discover when awareness serves action and when it creates paralysis. Through repeated cycles, they learn to be aware without being self-conscious.

### Line 4: Application
**Exalted (Moon):** The capacity to apply awareness in the now—to take what is recognized and manifest it through action. Contemplation that leads to embodiment.
**Detriment (Pluto):** Awareness without application, contemplating endlessly without ever acting, making presence theoretical rather than lived.

The fourth line transpersonal shift: at this line, the individual recognizes that **contemplation serves action**—that awareness is not the endpoint but the preparation for manifestation. The shadow is using observation to avoid engagement.

### Line 5: Realism
**Exalted (Mercury):** The gift of seeing reality as it is, not as one wishes it to be. Present-moment realism that grounds expression in truth.
**Detriment (Earth):** Cynical realism that refuses possibility, seeing only limitation, making the now a prison rather than an opening.

Line 5 is the heretic/savior dynamic applied to presence. The individual with this line carries a projection that their awareness of the now will **transform others**—that their realism will wake people up. When exalted, they see clearly and speak truth. When detriment, they impose limitation.

### Line 6: Wisdom
**Exalted (Venus):** The culmination of contemplation—wisdom that comes from sustained presence, from recognizing the now across countless moments until patterns emerge without losing each moment's uniqueness.
**Detriment (Mars):** False wisdom that speaks from accumulated past rather than present recognition, confusing memory with awareness.

The sixth line culminates in **embodied wisdom**. After the trials of lines 1-5, the individual recognizes that **true wisdom is always in the now**—that past experience informs but does not replace present awareness. This is the wisdom that speaks from being, not from knowing.

## Circuitry Analysis

Gate 20 belongs to the **Integration Circuit**, one of three circuits within the broader Individual stream (Knowing, Centering, and Integration). The Integration Circuit is characterized by:

- **Format Energy:** The capacity to integrate experience into a coherent self
- **Individual Empowerment:** Demonstrating what is possible through embodied presence
- **Existential Awareness:** Recognition of being here, in the now, in this body
- **No Teaching Obligation:** Integration serves self-realization, not collective instruction

**The Role of Gate 20 in the Circuit:**

Gate 20 is the **voice of the now** within the Integration Circuit—the Throat Center's capacity to *express* present-moment awareness. However, this gate can connect to three different gates (10, 34, 57), creating three distinct expressions of contemplation:

- **Channel 20-10 (Awakening):** Present awareness connected to self-love and behavior (G Center)
- **Channel 20-34 (Charisma):** Present awareness connected to pure power (Sacral)
- **Channel 20-57 (The Brain Wave):** Present awareness connected to intuitive clarity (Spleen)

Each channel creates a different flavor of "the now" being expressed.

**Why Individual Integration Requires Presence:**

Because the Integration Circuit is designed to **empower through embodied individuality**, Gate 20's present-moment awareness is essential—it ensures that expression comes from *being* rather than from should, could, or would. The individual speaks from the now, not from concept.

## Biological and Somatic Dimension

Gate 20 is located in the **Throat Center**, which corresponds biologically to the **thyroid and parathyroid glands**—the metabolic regulators that govern energy expression, manifestation, and communication.

#### Physiological Signature

Individuals with Gate 20 defined may experience:
- A **somatic pull into the now**—the body relaxes into presence, awareness drops into embodiment
- **Thyroid sensitivity** when presence is interrupted or when expression is forced rather than spontaneous
- A felt sense of **"hereness"**—the recognition that "I am here, now, in this body, in this moment"
- **Speech that arises from presence**—words that emerge from being rather than from planning

#### Energetic Signature

The Throat Center radiates **manifestation and expression** into the aura. When Gate 20 is active, the aura carries a frequency that says: "I am fully present in this moment, and my presence is expressed naturally." Others may feel this as **grounding**, **authentic**, or (if the individual is in Not-Self) **detached** or **self-absorbed**.

**Defined vs. Undefined in Gate 20:**

- **Defined Gate 20:** Consistent capacity for present-moment awareness and expression. The individual knows they are here to be in the now and to manifest that presence.
- **Undefined/Open Gate 20:** Samples presence from others. Vulnerable to conditioning around "needing to be present" or "performing mindfulness." Can become wise about the now by observing it in others.

## Strategy Integration
Gate 20, being in the Throat Center, does not carry Authority (only Sacral, Solar Plexus, Splenic, Ego, Self-Projected, and No-Inner-Authority are Authority centers). However, **it is a manifestation center**, which means it impacts how decisions are expressed into action.

**For Generators/Manifesting Generators with Gate 20:**

The expression of the now must still wait for the Sacral response. The Gate 20 energy says "This is what's happening!" but the Sacral must respond to confirm whether *speaking* or *acting* from this awareness is correct. Not every observation needs to be shared.

**For Projectors with Gate 20:**

The presence is clear, but the Projector must wait for invitation before sharing their awareness of the now. Uninvited expression of what is can lead to bitterness. The gift is knowing *when* to speak from presence and *when* to remain silent.

**For Manifestors with Gate 20:**

The awareness of the now can be expressed, but the Manifestor must **inform** before manifesting actions based on present recognition. Informing allows others to prepare for what the Manifestor sees and will act upon.

**For Reflectors with Gate 20:**

The presence in the now is inconsistent (activated only by transits or others). The Reflector must wait the full lunar cycle before committing to actions based on momentary awareness, ensuring the recognition aligns with their deep timing.

## Not-Self Patterns
**Shadow Expressions of Gate 20:**

- **Superficiality:** Speaking and acting from surface awareness, skimming the now without truly dwelling in it. Performative presence.
- **Dogmatism:** Imposing past frameworks on present reality, refusing to see what is actually happening in favor of what should be happening.
- **Self-Consciousness:** Observing oneself so intensely that spontaneous presence becomes impossible, creating paralysis disguised as awareness.
- **Detachment:** Using "presence" as an excuse to avoid engagement, contemplating without applying, observing without acting.
- **Spiritual Bypassing:** Using "being in the now" to avoid responsibility, planning, or consequence—presence as escape rather than engagement.
- **Compulsive Expression:** Speaking every observation, sharing every moment of awareness, overwhelming others with unfiltered now-ness.

#### Not-Self Indicators

- Frustration from speaking without Sacral response (for Generators/MGs)
- Bitterness from sharing awareness without invitation (for Projectors)
- Feeling disconnected from the now despite speaking about presence
- Burnout from performing presence rather than embodying it

**Conditioning Patterns for Undefined Gate 20:**

Those without Gate 20 may be conditioned to believe:
- "I must be present and mindful to be spiritual or awake."
- "I need to express every moment's awareness to prove I'm conscious."
- "If I'm not in the now, I'm failing at life."

This conditioning leads to forcing presence in areas that are not mechanically theirs to embody, or feeling inadequate despite having other forms of awareness.

## Deconditioning Process

The **7-year deconditioning cycle** for Gate 20 involves learning to recognize when presence is authentic (natural embodiment of the now) versus conditioned (performed mindfulness or spiritual bypassing).

**Year 1-2:** Awareness phase—noticing when presence feels genuine versus when it feels like performance, obligation, or escape from responsibility.

**Year 3-4:** Experimentation phase—exploring different forms of expression from the now, observing which manifestations serve and which create resistance.

**Year 5-6:** Refinement phase—beginning to trust natural presence, honoring when to express awareness and when to simply be without articulation.

**Year 7:** Integration phase—presence becomes aligned with Strategy and Authority. The individual expresses the now from **self-assurance** (Gene Keys Gift) rather than superficiality. Awareness serves embodiment, not performance.

#### Somatic Practice

- Notice where the impulse to express awareness arises in the body. Is it the throat (genuine manifestation)? The mind (mental pressure)? The gut (Sacral response)?
- Practice **being in the now without needing to speak about it**—presence does not require performance.
- Honor **spontaneous expression**—when awareness flows into voice naturally, without force, trust it.
