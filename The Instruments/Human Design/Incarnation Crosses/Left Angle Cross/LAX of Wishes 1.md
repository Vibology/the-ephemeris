# Left Angle Cross of Wishes (1)

## Gates: 3/50/41/31 | Quarter of Initiation

### Ra's Keynote
> "Altruistic, seductive, and influential people always looking for possibilities which make life better for others; seeing and re-ordering things in a new way."[^1]

---

## The Four Gates

### Personality Sun: Gate 3 - Difficulty at the Beginning (Ordering)
**Center:** Sacral | **Circuitry:** Individual Knowing

Gate 3 is the **power to bring order from chaos**—the life force that sustains innovation through the difficulty of new beginnings. As the Personality Sun, this defines the conscious purpose: to see and re-order things in new ways that have never existed before.

### Personality Earth: Gate 50 - The Cauldron (Values)
**Center:** Spleen | **Circuitry:** Tribal Ego (Defense)

Gate 50 grounds the innovative impulse with **instinctual values about tribal well-being**. This custodianship ensures that the new ordering serves the collective, that innovation is anchored in what genuinely preserves and improves life.

### Design Sun: Gate 41 - Decrease (Contraction)
**Center:** Root | **Circuitry:** Collective Abstract

Gate 41 operates unconsciously as **the pressure to imagine new experiences**—the only gate designed to initiate the experiential process. This fuels the cross with endless fantasies of what could be, the "what if" that drives the search for possibilities.

### Design Earth: Gate 31 - Influence (Leading)
**Center:** Throat | **Circuitry:** Collective Logic

Gate 31 grounds the fantasy pressure with **the voice of democratic leadership**. The wishes find articulation through influential speech that can organize and direct collective action toward better possibilities.

---

## Cross Theme

The **LAX of Wishes (1)** in the Quarter of Initiation carries the archetype of the **trans-personal visionary reformer**—one whose karmic purpose involves re-ordering reality to make life better for others.

As a **Left Angle Cross**, this is a trans-personal destiny. The individual does not live primarily for themselves but carries a karmic contract to envision and articulate better possibilities for those they encounter. Their seductiveness and influence are vehicles for altruistic transformation.

This cross bridges four circuits:
- **Individual Knowing** (3): Mutation and innovation
- **Tribal Ego/Defense** (50): Values and custodianship
- **Collective Abstract** (41): Fantasy and experiential pressure
- **Collective Logic** (31): Democratic leadership and influence

In the Quarter of Initiation (Mind), this Wishes uses mental faculties to:
- Process innovative visions into communicable form
- Understand which new orderings genuinely serve collective well-being
- Discover the patterns that transform fantasy into achievable possibility
- Lead through articulated insight that inspires collective action

The trans-personal nature means these wishes are not personal fantasies alone—they are meant to seduce and influence others toward better ways of being.

---

## Quarter Context

**Quarter of Initiation | Realm of Alcyone | Purpose through Mind**

In this quarter, the Left Angle Cross of Wishes fulfills its karmic purpose through **mental processing of possibility**. The pressure to imagine (Gate 41) and the power to innovate (Gate 3) are transmuted through the mind into influential leadership (Gate 31) grounded in tribal values (Gate 50).

The 5/1, 5/2, 6/2, and 6/3 profiles associated with Left Angle Crosses bring their own dynamics:
- **5/1 and 5/2**: The heretic/savior projected upon to know what is possible, called in when the collective needs new direction
- **6/2 and 6/3**: The role model who demonstrates through lived example how to re-order life for the better, becoming the elder visionary

---

## Shadow Expression

- **Seductive manipulation:** Using the altruistic appearance to serve personal agendas rather than collective improvement
- **Fantasy without grounding:** Endless imagination of possibilities without the tribal values to anchor them in real well-being
- **Innovation for its own sake:** Re-ordering things in new ways that do not actually make life better
- **Influential but uncommitted:** Articulating possibilities without the life force to sustain them through difficulty
- **Utopian projection:** Wishing for impossible perfection, creating disappointment rather than achievable improvement

---

## Highest Expression

- **Altruistic vision:** Genuinely seeing and creating possibilities that make life better for others
- **Grounded innovation:** Re-ordering things in new ways that are anchored in instinctual tribal values
- **Seductive inspiration:** Using natural influence to draw others toward better possibilities
- **Karmic service:** Fulfilling the trans-personal destiny by entering relationships that need visionary re-ordering
- **Sustainable transformation:** Having the life force (Gate 3) to sustain innovation through its difficult beginnings
