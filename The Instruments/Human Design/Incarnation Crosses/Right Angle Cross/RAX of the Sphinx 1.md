# Right Angle Cross of the Sphinx (1)

## Gates: 13/7/1/2 | Quarter of Initiation

### Ra's Keynote
> "People with an individual direction whose unique way of remembering experiences brings a living, vibrant past into the future."[^1]

---

## The Four Gates

### Personality Sun: Gate 13 - The Fellowship of Man (The Listener)
**Center:** G Center | **Circuitry:** Collective Sharing

Gate 13 is the **listener**—the capacity to witness, receive, and hold the stories of human experience. As the Personality Sun, this defines the conscious purpose: to be a witness to collective memory, holding space for what has been experienced.

### Personality Earth: Gate 7 - The Army (The Role of the Self)
**Center:** G Center | **Circuitry:** Collective Sharing

Gate 7 grounds the listening with **leadership and direction**. This is the capacity to point the way, to demonstrate direction through example. The Sphinx listens in order to guide.

### Design Sun: Gate 1 - The Creative (Self-Expression)
**Center:** G Center | **Circuitry:** Individual Knowing

Gate 1 operates unconsciously as **creative self-expression**—the drive toward unique individual direction. This fuels the Sphinx with creative energy seeking authentic expression.

### Design Earth: Gate 2 - The Receptive (The Direction of the Self)
**Center:** G Center | **Circuitry:** Individual Knowing

Gate 2 grounds the creativity with **receptivity to direction**. The Sphinx unconsciously receives direction from a deeper source, allowing purpose to emerge rather than forcing it.

---

## Cross Theme

The **RAX of the Sphinx (1)** in the Quarter of Initiation carries the archetype of the **mental witness**—one who fulfills purpose through mind by remembering, listening, and bringing the past forward.

All four gates reside in the **G Center**, making this a pure identity cross. The Sphinx holds the mystery of direction itself—where we come from and where we are going.

In the Quarter of Initiation (Mind), this Sphinx uses mental faculties to:
- Remember and hold collective experiences
- Provide direction through witnessing
- Express unique individual knowing
- Receive guidance receptively

The Sphinx doesn't give easy answers. They listen, hold, witness—and through this, direction emerges.

---

## Quarter Context

**Quarter of Initiation | Realm of Alcyone | Purpose through Mind**

As Cross #1 in this quarter, the Sphinx (1) establishes the foundation: all mental purpose begins with **witnessing and remembering**. Before we can think clearly about the future, we must understand what has come before.

The Sphinx poses the eternal question: "What is the purpose of remembering?" The answer: to bring a living, vibrant past into service of the future.

---

## Shadow Expression

- **Listening fatigue:** Overwhelmed by collective stories, withdrawing into isolation
- **Direction without foundation:** Attempting to lead without having truly witnessed
- **Creative blockage:** Gate 1's expression stifled by over-identification with others' stories
- **Cryptic opacity:** Becoming so enigmatic that connection is lost
- **Mental noise:** Remembering becomes rumination rather than wisdom

---

## Highest Expression

- **Sacred witness:** Holding space for the full spectrum of human experience
- **Living memory:** Bringing the past forward in service of collective understanding
- **Quiet leadership:** Direction emerging from presence rather than proclamation
- **Creative receptivity:** Unique expression arising from deep listening
- **The riddler:** Posing questions that invite others into their own mysteries
